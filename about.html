About InternSpark

InternSpark is a next-generation internship and skill development platform designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry experience.

Founder & Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Bhanupratap Biswas

Founder & CEO | EdTech | Internship & Skill Development

Bhanupratap Biswas is the Founder & CEO of InternSpark, an online internship and skill development platform focused on bridging the gap between academic education and real-world industry experience. He leads the strategic vision, platform development, and operations of InternSpark with a strong emphasis on practical, project-based learning and career readiness.

Under his leadership, InternSpark delivers structured online internships, skill-focused training programs, and verified credentials designed to empower students and early professionals across India with meaningful industry exposure and employable skills.
